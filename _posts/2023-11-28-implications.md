# Math Implications

$$ p \to q$$
$$ q \to v $$
, portanto:
$$ p \to v $$

| $$p$$ | $$q$$ | $$ p \to q$$ |
| :---: | :---: | :----------: |
|   F   |   F   |      T       |
|   F   |   T   |      T       |
|   T   |   F   |      F       |
|   T   |   T   |      T       |

$$ p \to q \equiv \neg p \lor q$$
$$ \neg q \to \neg p \equiv \neg p \lor q$$
$$ p \to q \equiv \neg q \to \neg p $$
